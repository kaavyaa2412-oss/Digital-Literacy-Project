***Social Media Checklist for College Students***

**DO’s:**  
1\. Think before you post.  
2\. Use respectful and polite language.  
3\. Keep your profile professional.  
4\. Protect your personal information.  
5\. Verify information before sharing.

**DON’Ts:**  
1\. Do not share fake or unverified news.  
2\. Do not post offensive or abusive content.  
3\. Do not overshare personal details.  
4\. Do not engage in online arguments or trolling.  
5\. Do not ignore privacy settings.
