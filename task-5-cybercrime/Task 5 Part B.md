***Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students***

1\. Never share your OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone.  
2\. Do not click on suspicious links or unknown messages.  
3\. Always verify the sender before responding to emails or calls.  
4\. Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.  
5\. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.  
6\. Do not approve unknown UPI payment requests (you might lose money).  
7\. Remember: to receive money, you do NOT need to enter a UPI PIN.  
8\. Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history.  
9\. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.  
10\. Keep your apps and devices updated for better security.
